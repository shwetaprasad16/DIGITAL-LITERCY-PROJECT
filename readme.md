Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project focuses on building essential online skills required for students in today’s digital world. It includes awareness about internet safety, professional communication, coding platforms, and cybercrime prevention. The project is divided into five practical tasks that enhance both technical and responsible digital behavior.
Tasks Included
Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created an infographic highlighting key aspects of digital literacy such as online safety, privacy protection, and responsible internet usage. It visually represents important do’s and don’ts for users.
Task 2: Student Digital Portfolio
Developed a personal digital portfolio to showcase academic details, skills, and projects. This helps in building an online presence and professional identity.
Task 3: Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Explored platforms like coding websites and collaborative tools. Practiced problem-solving and learned how developers collaborate and share code efficiently.
Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
Drafted a guide explaining how to write professional emails and follow proper online communication etiquette. Included email formats, do’s and don’ts, and social media responsibility.
Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
Studied cybercrime cases such as cyberbullying and phishing. Created a prevention guide to help users stay safe online and protect their digital identity
