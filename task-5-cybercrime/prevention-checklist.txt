Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)
✅ General Safety Tips
	•	Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable 2-factor authentication (2FA).
	•	Do not share personal information (address, college ID, phone number) publicly.
	•	Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.
	•	Verify job offers, internships, and scholarships before applying.
	•	Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
	•	Log out from shared/public computers after use.
	•	Be careful while using public Wi-Fi; avoid accessing sensitive accounts.
💸 Financial / UPI Safety
	•	Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone—even if they claim to be from the bank.
	•	Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept random “collect requests” on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
	•	Double-check payment details before sending money.
	•	Immediately report unauthorized transactions to your bank.
🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
	•	Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
	•	Call Cybercrime Helpline: 1930
	•	Save evidence like screenshots, messages, and transaction details.
✔ Stay alert, think before you click, and protect your digital identity.
