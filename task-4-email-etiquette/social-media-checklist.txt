Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist (For College Students)
✅ Do’s
	•	Think before posting; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
	•	Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
	•	Adjust privacy settings to control who can see your posts
	•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
	•	Maintain a positive and professional online presenceT
❌ Don’ts
	•	Don’t share personal or sensitive information publicly
	•	Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
	•	Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
	•	Don’t accept friend/follow requests from unknown people
	•	Don’t click on suspicious links or messages
✔ Follow these guidelines to stay safe, responsible, and professional online.
